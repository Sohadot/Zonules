## Status

This standard governs the `/acquire/` surface and any communication that surrounds it. It is subordinate to `ASSET_THESIS.md`. It exists because the acquisition surface is the one place where a reference asset can quietly destroy its own authority — by sounding like a domain listing. This standard forbids that.

The governing principle is drawn directly from the asset's own thesis:

> A seeing system is trusted because of the hidden structure that holds its focus. An asset is acquired because of the hidden structure that holds its value. The acquisition surface must show the structure, not beg for the sale.

---

## The Inevitability Posture

The asset does not ask to be bought. It demonstrates why not owning it is a structural gap. The buyer must arrive at the loss themselves, from evidence, not from pressure.

This follows the Loss-in-Not-Acquiring principle: a strategic buyer should feel that the category language, ontology, standard, engine, governance record, and accumulated reference cannot be rebuilt on a deadline — and that leaving them in someone else's hands is the real cost.

---

## What the Surface Must Do

1. State plainly that Zonules.com is a developed category asset — a Category Intelligence Factory — not a domain for sale.
2. Show the structure that constitutes the asset: the governing thesis, the three-layer reference architecture, the Focus Integrity Ontology and Standard, the Focus Integrity Engine, the governance and decision record, the multilingual architecture, and the static-first security posture.
3. Name buyer *categories*, never individual buyers.
4. Offer one calm, institutional contact path: `agent@sohadot.com`.
5. Remain readable as reference-grade material — the same voice as every other surface.

---

## Absolute Prohibitions (enforced by the quality gate)

The acquisition surface must never contain:

- Price figures, currency symbols, or numeric valuations
- Auction, bidding, "make an offer", "buy now", or marketplace framing
- Urgency or scarcity language: "act now", "limited time", "hurry", "don't miss", "won't last", countdowns
- Public naming of any specific prospective buyer or company as a target
- Discount, deal, or sale framing
- Any claim that the asset *needs* to be sold

These are checked automatically. A single violation blocks publication of the surface (Quality Gate item 15).

---

## Voice

Calm. Institutional. Evidence-based. Strategically restrained. The acquisition surface may be confident. It may not be eager. It may state value. It may not chase it.

> The asset attracts strategic inquiry through authority. It does not generate artificial urgency.

---

## Governing Sentences

> **Show the structure, not the price.**

> **Name the category, never the buyer.**

> **The asset must not need the acquisition to succeed.**
