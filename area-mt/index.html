Area MT (Middle Temporal)

The cortical specialist for visual motion.

Definition

Area MT, also called V5, is a cortical region specialized for processing visual motion.

Scientific Grounding

Most neurons in MT are tuned to the direction and speed of moving stimuli. Damage to MT can impair the perception of motion while leaving other vision largely intact.

*Sources: SRC-013, SRC-009. See the source registry for classification.*

Anatomical Role

Area MT is an interpretation-layer specialist: it reads motion as a distinct dimension of meaning in the scene.
