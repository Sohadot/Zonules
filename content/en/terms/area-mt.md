# Area MT (Middle Temporal)

> The cortical specialist for visual motion.

## Definition

Area MT, also called V5, is a cortical region specialized for processing visual motion. [CLM-928]

## Scientific Grounding

Most neurons in MT are tuned to the direction and speed of moving stimuli. [CLM-929] Damage to MT can impair the perception of motion while leaving other vision largely intact. [CLM-930]

*Sources: SRC-013, SRC-009. See the source registry for classification.*

## Anatomical Role

Area MT is an interpretation-layer specialist: it reads motion as a distinct dimension of meaning in the scene. [CLM-931]
