# Binocular Rivalry

> When two signals cannot be reconciled, one is read.

## Definition

Binocular rivalry is the alternation in perception that occurs when the two eyes view incompatible images. [CLM-996]

## Scientific Grounding

Rather than blending, awareness switches between the two images over time. [CLM-997] Rivalry is studied as a window into how the brain selects a single conscious percept. [CLM-998]

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-014. See the source registry for classification.*

## Perceptual Role

Binocular rivalry is a separation-layer conflict: when two signals cannot be reconciled, only one is read at a time. [CLM-999]
