# Color Perception

> Color is assigned by the system, not read directly from light.

## Definition

Color perception is the visual system's construction of color from the spectral composition of light. [CLM-280]

## Scientific Grounding

Trichromatic cone signals are processed into opponent color channels. [CLM-281] Perceived color depends on context and surrounding illumination, an effect known as color constancy. [CLM-282]

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-006. See the source registry for classification.*

## Perceptual Role

Color perception is an interpretation (FIO-05): color is assigned by the system, not a property read directly from light. [CLM-283]
