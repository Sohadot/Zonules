# Cue Integration

> Several weak signals fused into one confident reading.

## Definition

Cue integration is the combination of multiple, separately ambiguous cues into a single more reliable percept. [CLM-972]

## Scientific Grounding

Cues are typically weighted by their reliability, with more reliable cues dominating the result. [CLM-973] Optimal integration can yield a percept more precise than any single cue alone. [CLM-974]

*Sources: SRC-006, SRC-005. See the source registry for classification.*

## Perceptual Role

Cue integration is an interpretation-layer synthesis: several weak signals are fused into one confident reading. [CLM-975]
