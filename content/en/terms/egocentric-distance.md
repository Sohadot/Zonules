# Egocentric Distance

> The world placed relative to the self that views it.

## Definition

Egocentric distance is the perceived distance of an object from the observer. [CLM-1036]

## Scientific Grounding

It relies on cues such as height in the visual field, familiar size, and the ground surface. [CLM-1037] Egocentric distance judgments tend to compress at greater distances. [CLM-1038]

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-006. See the source registry for classification.*

## Perceptual Role

Egocentric distance is an interpretation-layer measure: it places the world relative to the self that views it. [CLM-1039]
