# Ensemble Perception

> The crowd summarized before any one face is read.

## Definition

Ensemble perception is the visual system's ability to summarize sets of similar items by their statistics, such as average size or orientation. [CLM-716]

## Scientific Grounding

Observers can report the mean of a group accurately even when they cannot report any individual member. [CLM-717] This summary representation provides a compact description of crowded or cluttered scenes. [CLM-718]

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-006. See the source registry for classification.*

## Perceptual Role

Ensemble perception is a separation-layer compression — it extracts stable structure from many noisy elements. [CLM-719]
