# Evidence Accumulation

> Meaning reached by integrating signal over time.

## Definition

Evidence accumulation is the gradual gathering of noisy sensory information toward a perceptual decision. [CLM-980]

## Scientific Grounding

Sequential-sampling models describe how evidence builds until it crosses a decision boundary. [CLM-981] The rate of accumulation reflects the quality of the sensory signal. [CLM-982]

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-006. See the source registry for classification.*

## Perceptual Role

Evidence accumulation is an interpretation-layer process: meaning is reached by integrating signal over time, not in an instant. [CLM-983]
