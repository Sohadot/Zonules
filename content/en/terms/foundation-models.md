# Vision Foundation Models

> A general visual base many tasks are built on.

## Definition

Vision foundation models are large models pretrained on broad data that can be adapted to many downstream tasks. [CLM-1188]

## Scientific Grounding

Pretraining at scale lets them transfer to new tasks with little task-specific data. [CLM-1189] The same broad pretraining that gives them generality can also propagate biases from the training data. [CLM-1190]

*Sources: SRC-019, SRC-022, SRC-017. See the source registry for classification.*

## Machine-Vision Role

Foundation models are a suspension-layer base: they hold a general visual representation that many tasks are built upon. [CLM-1191]
