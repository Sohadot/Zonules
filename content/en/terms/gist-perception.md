# Gist Perception

> The meaning of a scene grasped in a single glance.

## Definition

Gist perception is the rapid extraction of the overall meaning of a scene from a single brief glance. [CLM-712]

## Scientific Grounding

Observers can categorize a scene as a beach, street, or forest within roughly a tenth of a second, before identifying individual objects. [CLM-713] The gist constrains expectations about which objects are likely present and where. [CLM-714]

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-006. See the source registry for classification.*

## Perceptual Role

Gist perception is an interpretation-layer shortcut — meaning grasped before detail is resolved. [CLM-715]
