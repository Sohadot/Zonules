# Global Precedence

> The forest is seen before the trees that compose it.

## Definition

Global precedence is the tendency to perceive the overall form of a configuration before its local parts. [CLM-736]

## Scientific Grounding

In hierarchical figures, where a large letter is made of small different letters, the global letter is usually identified faster. [CLM-737] Global structure can interfere with local judgments more than local structure interferes with global ones. [CLM-738]

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-006. See the source registry for classification.*

## Perceptual Role

Global precedence is an interpretation-layer ordering — the whole is read before the parts. [CLM-739]
