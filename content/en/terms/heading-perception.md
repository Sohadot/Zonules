# Heading Perception

> Direction of travel read from motion.

## Definition

Heading perception is the judgment of one's direction of travel from the visual motion of the scene. [CLM-1016]

## Scientific Grounding

The focus of expansion in the optic-flow field indicates the direction of heading. [CLM-1017] Observers can judge heading accurately from optic flow alone. [CLM-1018]

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-006. See the source registry for classification.*

## Perceptual Role

Heading perception is an interpretation-layer inference: direction of travel read from the structure of motion. [CLM-1019]
