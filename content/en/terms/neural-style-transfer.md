# Neural Style Transfer

> Appearance detached from source and reassigned.

## Definition

Neural style transfer recomposes the content of one image in the visual style of another. [CLM-1084]

## Scientific Grounding

It separates and recombines content and style representations extracted from a convolutional network. [CLM-1085] It was an early demonstration that learned features can be manipulated to generate new imagery. [CLM-1086]

*Sources: SRC-017, SRC-007. See the source registry for classification.*
