# Ora Serrata

> The serrated rim where the seeing retina ends.

## Definition

The ora serrata is the serrated junction where the light-sensitive retina ends and the ciliary body begins. [CLM-856]

## Scientific Grounding

It marks the anterior boundary of the neural retina. [CLM-857] The peripheral retina near the ora serrata is thin and contains few photoreceptors. [CLM-858]

*Sources: SRC-001, SRC-002. See the source registry for classification.*

## Anatomical Role

The ora serrata is a suspension-layer boundary: it defines the structural edge of the surface on which images are formed. [CLM-859]
