# Parafovea

> The ring around the center where detail falls away.

## Definition

The parafovea is the ring of retina immediately surrounding the fovea, containing a mix of cones and a rising density of rods. [CLM-620]

## Scientific Grounding

Spatial acuity falls steeply from the fovea through the parafovea toward the periphery. [CLM-621] The parafovea contributes to the detection of detail just outside the point of fixation. [CLM-622]

*Sources: SRC-011, SRC-012. See the source registry for classification.*

## Anatomical Role

The parafovea is a separation-layer gradient: it marks how quickly the retina trades resolution for coverage away from the center. [CLM-623]
