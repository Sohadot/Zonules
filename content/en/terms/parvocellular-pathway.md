# Parvocellular Pathway

> The detailed stream tuned to what.

## Definition

The parvocellular pathway is a visual stream, originating in midget ganglion cells, specialized for fine detail and color. [CLM-896]

## Scientific Grounding

It conducts signals more slowly and sustains its response, supporting high spatial resolution. [CLM-897] It occupies the four dorsal parvocellular layers of the lateral geniculate nucleus. [CLM-898]

*Sources: SRC-009, SRC-013. See the source registry for classification.*

## Anatomical Role

The parvocellular pathway is a separation-layer channel tuned to what: fine, colored, detailed signal. [CLM-899]
