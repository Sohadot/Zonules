# Visual Scene Parsing

> Assigning structure and meaning to a scene introduces interpretation risk even when the optics are clean.

## Definition

Visual scene parsing involves simultaneous segmentation of figure from ground, grouping of elements into objects, and assignment of spatial relationships between objects. [CLM-188]

## Scientific Grounding

Gist perception — the rapid, holistic extraction of a scene's category and dominant properties within the first 100 ms of viewing — precedes detailed object-level parsing. [CLM-189] Scene parsing is influenced by both bottom-up salience (contrast, motion, novelty) and top-down knowledge (scene schemas, expected object locations). [CLM-190]

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-006. See the source registry for classification.*
