# Visual Search

> Focus held and moved deliberately until the target is found.

## Definition

Visual search is the task of locating a target among distractors in a visual scene. [CLM-288]

## Scientific Grounding

Search can be efficient and parallel or inefficient and serial, depending on target-distractor similarity. [CLM-289] Feature-based search differs in difficulty from conjunction search, where targets are defined by combinations of features. [CLM-290]

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-006. See the source registry for classification.*

## Perceptual Role

Visual search is a suspension of attention (FIO-01): focus is held and moved deliberately until the target is found. [CLM-291]
