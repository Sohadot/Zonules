# Visual Word Recognition

> Marks resolved into language almost without effort.

## Definition

Visual word recognition is the rapid identification of written words from their visual form. [CLM-1052]

## Scientific Grounding

Skilled readers recognize familiar words largely as wholes rather than letter by letter. [CLM-1053] Word recognition is faster for real words than for equally long random letter strings. [CLM-1054]

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-015. See the source registry for classification.*
