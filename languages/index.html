Languages

Translation is not copying. Every language that carries this reference system must carry it without loss of meaning, without loss of source traceability, and without loss of safety boundaries.

English: the frozen master

The English corpus of 300 pages was frozen in Sprint 6F (2026-06-14). The English master is the sole source of truth for every translation. No translated page may contradict a frozen English field.

  • All English routes, canonical URLs, and slugs are immutable
  • All claim IDs and source IDs are frozen
  • All FIO/FIS classifications and safety classes are frozen
  • All medical disclaimers are frozen
  • Additive hreflang metadata for launched translations is permitted

French: active core-expanded pilot (Sprint 7F)

French is at pilot/core-expanded status: a governed state between architecture-defined and fully launched. Fifty anchor and core pages are indexed and carry active hreflang. This does not constitute a full language launch.

The fifty French pages cover the three reference layers:

  • Layer 1 -- Anatomy: includes the original anatomy anchors plus Cristallin, Cornée, Disque optique, Champ visuel, Voie dorsale, and Voie ventrale.
  • Layer 2 -- Perception: includes Perception visuelle, Attention sélective, Acuité visuelle, Erreur perceptive, and Mémoire visuelle de travail.
  • Layer 3 -- Machine Vision: includes the original machine-vision anchors plus Modèles de diffusion, Authenticité du contenu, IA ophtalmique, IA en imagerie médicale, and Métriques d’évaluation.
  • Cross-layer -- Ontology: Integrite du Focus

A full French launch requires all 300 pages to be translated, validated, and governed by a DECISION_LOG.md entry.

Other languages: architecture defined

German, Spanish, Chinese, Japanese, and Russian have route structures defined in data/translation-map.json and are registered in data/languages.json. No content has been translated. No pages exist. No hreflang is emitted for these languages.

Creating content for an architecture-defined language requires a governance entry in DECISION_LOG.md before any page is created.

Translation governance policy

Every translation is governed by docs/MULTILINGUAL_ARCHITECTURE.md. Language-invariant fields must not change with translation: concept IDs, term IDs, FIO/FIS codes, safety classes, claim IDs, and source IDs.

Medical disclaimers, source attribution, and the "detection is not diagnosis" boundary must be preserved in every translation.

No partial language doctrine

A language is either launched with all governed pages complete or it is not. Pilot status is a governed exception allowing anchor page indexing before a full launch; it is not a workaround for the doctrine.

Validation

All translated content is validated by scripts/validate_multilingual.py and scripts/validate_all.py. These scripts enforce file existence, safety markers, FIO/FIS immutability, hreflang boundaries, Arabic RTL requirements, and visible language counts on this page.

Next phases

Following Sprint 7F, future work should audit the 50-page French core before any additional language expansion. A full-language launch remains blocked until all 300 governed pages pass validation.
