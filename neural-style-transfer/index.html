Neural Style Transfer

Appearance detached from source and reassigned.

Definition

Neural style transfer recomposes the content of one image in the visual style of another.

Scientific Grounding

It separates and recombines content and style representations extracted from a convolutional network. It was an early demonstration that learned features can be manipulated to generate new imagery.

*Sources: SRC-017, SRC-007. See the source registry for classification.*
