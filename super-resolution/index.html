Super-Resolution

The detail it adds is invention, not fact.

Definition

Super-resolution reconstructs a high-resolution image from a lower-resolution input.

Scientific Grounding

Learned methods hallucinate plausible detail that was not present in the low-resolution source. Because invented detail can look authentic, super-resolution outputs must not be treated as recovered ground truth.

*Sources: SRC-007, SRC-017, SRC-023. See the source registry for classification.*

Machine-Vision Role
