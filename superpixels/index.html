Superpixels

The image pre-grouped into coherent regions.

Definition

Superpixels are perceptually meaningful clusters of adjacent pixels that share color and texture.

Scientific Grounding

Grouping pixels into superpixels reduces the number of primitives later stages must process. They respect image boundaries, providing a more natural unit than a fixed pixel grid.

*Sources: SRC-007. See the source registry for classification.*

Machine-Vision Role

Superpixels are a separation-layer simplification: they pre-group the image into coherent regions before meaning is assigned.
