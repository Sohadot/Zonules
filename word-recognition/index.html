Visual Word Recognition

Marks resolved into language almost without effort.

Definition

Visual word recognition is the rapid identification of written words from their visual form.

Scientific Grounding

Skilled readers recognize familiar words largely as wholes rather than letter by letter. Word recognition is faster for real words than for equally long random letter strings.

*Sources: SRC-005, SRC-015. See the source registry for classification.*
